Tista Public data files

PathDescription
common/v1/countries.json List of the countries.
common/v1/population.json List of the countries population.
common/v1/us/states.json List of all US states.
common/v1/us/counties.json List of all US counties.
common/v1/us/population.json List of the US population by states and by counties.
common/v1/us/zips.json List of all US zips and counties grouped by zips.
common/v1/us/zip/2/222.json List of all US counties for the specific ZIPs group.
Note: common/v1/us/zip/<1st digit>/<1st-3d digist>.json

You can get data files in the CSV format, just replace file extension from the .json to the .csv.
